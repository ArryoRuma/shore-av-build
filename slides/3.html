Project Understanding

Current state assessment and strategic objectives for the sanctuary.

Current Context

The sanctuary currently operates with minimal infrastructure. The new design must bridge the gap to professional standards while respecting existing constraints.

Design-First Strategy

Our intent is to minimize risk and reduce ambiguity before installation begins.

  • Inform precise equipment selection

  • Establish accurate budget estimates

  • Create clear installation roadmap

Audio Performance

Prioritizing clear speech intelligibility for spoken word and reliable music reinforcement for the worship band.

Contemporary Worship

Enabling future-ready audiovisual capabilities including projection/IMAG and seamless livestream readiness.

Operational Simplicity

Systems designed for volunteer staff, balancing professional features with accessible control logic and workflows.
